3D Agent Scene for Previsualization
Anonymous Authors
3D Scene / Previsualization Under Review
A 3D-world-centric framework for generative previsualization that turns ideas into an editable, persistent scene shared across film, game, architecture, and urban-design workflows. Beyond producing visuals, it maintains a coherent world that can be inspected from new viewpoints, locally modified, and evolved over time, enabling fast yet controllable iteration between concept and production. The resulting pipeline supports rich downstream uses such as cinematic video creation and game-like interactive prototyping, pointing toward a more practical bridge between creative intent and high-quality 3D content.

World Scene Generation and Reconstruction
Anonymous Authors
3D World / Panorama Under Review
A unified single-panorama-to-3D-world system that turns one panoramic image into an explorable 3D scene. By tightly coupling view planning, novel-view panorama synthesis, and panorama-adapted 3D reconstruction within one pipeline, it expands scene coverage, recovers occluded and distant regions, and yields navigable 3D worlds with consistent geometry and appearance. This opens up applications in immersive content creation, virtual tours, world simulation, and downstream 3D-aware generation, where rich 3D environments need to be obtained from very limited visual capture.
